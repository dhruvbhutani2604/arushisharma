Who Am I?

I am a YouTuber & an International Fashion Business graduate from Nottingham Trent University. I am a visual storyteller that likes to bring ideas, opinions, identity, and expression to life through fashion-imagery and branding. My work includes digital marketing, marketing, and social media. I have been an active part of styling projects since 2018 and have recently branched into the field of PR and communications. I have worked in content creation and collaborated with multiple beauty & lifestyle brands. My skills include image curation, photography, videography, styling, branding, and PR.
